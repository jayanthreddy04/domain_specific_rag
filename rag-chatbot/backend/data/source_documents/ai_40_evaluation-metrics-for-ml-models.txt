Evaluation Metrics for ML Models
================================

Choosing metrics aligns with business goals and data characteristics. Imbalanced classification favors precision-recall over accuracy. Regression uses MAE, RMSE, and R-squared. Ranking tasks use MAP and NDCG. Calibration measures probability reliability. Fairness metrics assess subgroup performance. Statistical significance tests compare model variants. Holistic evaluation includes latency, cost, and maintainability.
