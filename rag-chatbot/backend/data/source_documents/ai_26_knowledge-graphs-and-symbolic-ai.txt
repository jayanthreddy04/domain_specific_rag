Knowledge Graphs and Symbolic AI
================================

Knowledge graphs represent entities and relationships in structured form. They support query answering, recommendation, and reasoning. Symbolic AI uses logic rules and ontologies. Neuro-symbolic approaches combine neural learning with explicit knowledge. Graph embeddings learn vector representations of nodes. RDF and SPARQL are semantic web standards. Enterprise knowledge management benefits from graph-augmented retrieval.
