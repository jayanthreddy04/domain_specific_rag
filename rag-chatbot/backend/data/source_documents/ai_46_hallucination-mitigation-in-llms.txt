Hallucination Mitigation in LLMs
================================

Hallucinations are plausible but incorrect LLM outputs. Mitigations include retrieval grounding, confidence calibration, and self-consistency checks. Constrained decoding enforces formats. Human review remains important for high-stakes domains. Evaluation benchmarks measure factual accuracy. Training on curated data and refusal behaviors reduces fabrication. Users should verify critical facts against authoritative sources.
