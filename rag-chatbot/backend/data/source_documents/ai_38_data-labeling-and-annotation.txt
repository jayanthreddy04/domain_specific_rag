Data Labeling and Annotation
============================

High-quality labels are critical for supervised learning success. Annotation platforms coordinate human labelers with guidelines. Active learning selects informative samples for labeling. Weak supervision uses noisy heuristics aggregated probabilistically. Inter-annotator agreement measures consistency. Costs scale with task complexity. Synthetic labels from simulations supplement real data in robotics and gaming.
