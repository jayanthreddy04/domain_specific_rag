AI Research Frontiers

Benchmarks like MMLU and HumanEval measure progress. Open problems include robust generalization, sample efficiency, and value alignment. Interdisciplinary collaboration with social scientists addresses societal impact. Publication peer review and reproducibility remain scientific norms.
