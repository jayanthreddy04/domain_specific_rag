Model Training and Optimization
===============================

Training neural models minimizes loss functions using gradient descent variants. Learning rate schedules and warmup stabilize convergence. Batch size affects memory and gradient noise. Mixed-precision training accelerates GPUs. Hyperparameter tuning uses grid search, random search, or Bayesian optimization. Early stopping halts training when validation loss plateaus. Distributed training scales across multiple devices for large models.
