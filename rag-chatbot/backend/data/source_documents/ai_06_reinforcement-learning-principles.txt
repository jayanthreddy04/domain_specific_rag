Reinforcement Learning Principles
=================================

Reinforcement learning trains agents to maximize cumulative reward in environments. Components include states, actions, policies, and value functions. Exploration versus exploitation is central. Algorithms include Q-learning, SARSA, policy gradients, and actor-critic methods. Deep reinforcement learning combines neural networks with RL for complex control tasks. Applications include game playing, robotics, and recommendation systems. Safety and reward hacking are important research areas.
