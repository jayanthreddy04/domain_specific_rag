Transformer Architecture
========================

Transformers replace recurrence with self-attention for sequence modeling. Each token attends to all others to capture long-range dependencies. Multi-head attention learns diverse relationships. Positional encodings inject order information. Encoder-decoder designs power translation; decoder-only models power text generation. Layer normalization and residual connections stabilize training. Transformers scale with data and parameters, enabling foundation models.
