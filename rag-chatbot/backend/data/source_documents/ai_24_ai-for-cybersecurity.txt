AI for Cybersecurity
====================

Machine learning enhances cybersecurity through anomaly detection, malware classification, and phishing identification. Models analyze network traffic patterns and endpoint logs. Adversarial attacks can evade classifiers, requiring robust training. SOAR platforms automate incident response workflows. False positives must be minimized to avoid alert fatigue. AI also enables offensive capabilities, raising dual-use concerns and defensive countermeasures.
